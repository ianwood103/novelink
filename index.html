Finally,
a home for new authors

With NoveLink, struggling new authors can finally make a name for themselves without having to get accepted by increasingly picky publishing companies. Users of NoveLink don't have to be authors themselves, as they can also use it to find new works of literature of their own and directly support their favorite authors, all for free.

Publish your own books,
for only $1.

As an author, the most important path to success is getting your works to your fans. However, adequate publishing deals can often be a long term time commitment and many new authors find themselves unable to get their career as an author going. With NoveLink, anyone who wants to publish their own works of literature can, with a one time payment of one dollar. This payment of one dollar not only goes towards supporting NoveLink but also makes sure all authors of NoveLink are at least somewhat serious about creating quality literature.

Directly support your favorite authors

Through NoveLink, fans of a particular NoveLink creator can follow their future projects and establish a 'feed' of sorts. This feed will include all of the author's works published on NoveLink, which a fan can access directly through the site completely for free. Fans can also directly donate to their favorite authors to support their career even more. NoveLink, however, is not completely limited to smaller authors that are looking to gain an audience, but it is also encouraged for already well-established authors to use the site to directly communicate with fans.

Create or join writers' guilds

Using NoveLink, fans of a particular group of authors, genre, or topic within literature can create writers' guilds. These guilds can serve as a themed discussion area in which multiple fans can easily communicate with eachother. Guilds can even create their own flags and raise funds to donate to certain authors within NoveLink. The more money a guild donates and the more members it has can give it special status, and can be featured on the Top Guilds page.
